About Me

Hi There!

I’m Ryan. A Vancouver-based, game designer and developer focusing on gameplay and level design, and development. With a passion for world-building and enhancing interaction between gamers I am fascinated by how a gaming medium can bring people together.

I am currently completing my fourth year studying game design and computing science. I’ve been developing analog and digital games for the past 4 years and am currently in the early stages of a new passion project I am developing with C# in the Unity game engine.

I can’t wait for the opportunities and challenges that will arise as I enter the game development field in the coming year.
