About Me

Hi There!

I’m Ryan. A Vancouver-based, Software Engineer and Game Designer focusing on gameplay design and development. As a lifelong learner with a passion for innovative technologies, I'm excited to see how the landscape of the technology sector continues to evolve.

I attended Simon Fraser University where I received a Bachelor of Science degree in Interactive Art and Technology and Computing Science, with a concentration in Interactive System Design. I’ve been developing games for the past 7 years and am currently working for This Game Studio as a Technical Game Designer, and studying software design principles.
